USA Service

We now have a serviced office in New York, USA. This office is here to act as a drop/distribution point for those wishing to sell bottles of Whisky that are located in the USA. These bottles will be photographed, stored and distributed from our New York Office.

Step one

Send your bottles to the service office where they will be photographed, stored and insured until the auction is complete.

Step two

If your bottle is successfully sold you will receive the funds and we will distribute your bottle to the buyer.

Step three

If your bottle is not sold. We can store it for you and repost on the next auction or we can organise for your bottle to be sent back to yourself. All the same terms and conditions apply, the same fee structure and delivery dates.

Step four

If your mailing you bottle send to:
